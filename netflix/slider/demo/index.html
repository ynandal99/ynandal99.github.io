Netflix Recommendation Engine


Do Netflix thumbnail suggestions vary by gender?

This group study was conducted by 20 students of the Business Intelligence & Analytics Class of 2021 where each participant tried to bring in as many votes as possible (including family members sharing the same Netflix account).

Key findings & statistics are given below: (you can download the source file here.)

  • - Total Votes - 459 Male and 388 Female Votes
  • - Total Titles - 50
  • - 56% of Females were shown Intimate thumbnails compared with 37% Males in 'Romantic' titles
  • - 32% of Males were shown Ominous thumbnails compared with 26% Females across all titles
